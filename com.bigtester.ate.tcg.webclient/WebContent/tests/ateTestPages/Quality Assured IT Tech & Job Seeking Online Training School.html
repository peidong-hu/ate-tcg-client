Course Brief Introduction

 

These courses are designed for job seekers, testers, developers, managers and end-user who want to learn how to assure the quality of the computer software on the delivery.

 

The courses lay a foundation in the principles of software quality and quality assurance.  Then, techniques are presented that can make QA an effective force in different size organizations.

 

Introduction to software QA and Testing will help the audience understand the larger picture of software quality factors, methodologies, and become more comfortable and confident in testing software applications at just about any level of detail: unit, integration, system, and user acceptance, by learning how to review requirement specification, how to develop test plans/test cases, how tools can help perform testing and how to measure software quality.

 

Two types of most popular software applications and their specific testing techniques are introduced and practiced in the courses to help the audience quickly get ready for the market.

 

Specific skills and tips are presented to help the audience to prepare their resumes and interviews for SQA job seeking.

 

Leve 3 training helps people obtain an advanced level qualification, have a broad understanding of testing in general, and an in depth understanding in a specific QA area. An in-depth understanding means sufficient knowledge of QA theory and practice to be able to influence the direction that an organization and/or project takes when creating, implementing and executing QA activities related to the specific area.

 

Private Tutoring courses are highly customized for trainees equipped with different knowledge background. These courses are designed to maximize customer's cost effectiveness and study-time efficiency. Class has only max 3 students and training location is flexible according to customers' need.
