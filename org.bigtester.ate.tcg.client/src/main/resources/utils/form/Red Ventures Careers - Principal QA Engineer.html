Principal QA Engineer





Your Impact:

As a Principal QA Engineer with the Imagitas team, you will work within a fast paced agile testing team to ensure our highest revenue generating applications are at the utmost quality possible by leveraging manual and automation test strategies. Your career as a Principal QA Engineer at Imagitas is highly visible and will help you gain exposure to critical applications being used by millions of external consumers each month on a live 24/7 basis.  

 This opportunity allows for career growth and learning and development opportunities within our IT group.  This position is located in Waltham, MA.

 

The Opportunity:

  • Define test automation strategies and plans for various projects, review product specifications, contribute to test harness, automate tests, execute test plans, and participate in defining/selecting appropriate test tools in delivering a high quality product.
  • Help with the day-to-day coordination between QA team members working on different projects – primarily related to Mover’s Guide Online
  • Enhance, maintain and execute Selenium automated scripts & run performance/load tests as needed.
  • Evaluate the current automation framework and practices within the team and improve it in term of resources, skill-sets, tools, environments, efficiency, and value
  • Help with daily deployments of various builds in test environments.
  • Work directly with analysts and developers to investigate software defects and accurately and clearly reports those defects in Jira.
  • Review requirements and specifications early in the project cycle and estimate/negotiate/plan for the scope for QA to commit to within upcoming sprint.
  • Work closely and collaborate with various groups: Product Managers, QA, Developers, DBA, System engineers in the context of an agile sprint.

 

Apply Now if you have:

  • Bachelor’s Degree with concentration in a technical related area such as Information Systems or Computer Science.
  • 7-10 years QA experience testing web based applications in a J2EE /Java environment working within formal QA test cycles (Agile with short cycle iterative releases highly preferable).
  • Proven experience with test automation tools such as
  • Experience planning and executing performance/load testing using tools such as LoadRunner, JMeter, or any other commercial/open source tool.
  • Experience with Continuous Integration tools like Jenkins and/or Bamboo is a plus.
  • Comfortable working with Agile development and testing methodologies such as Scrum is highly preferred.
  • Experience with SDLC and Quality Assurance/Software Testing best practices
  • Familiarity with Web Application Development technologies (XML, CSS, JavaScript, etc.).
  • Experience writing and executing SQL queries.
  • Must be a team player and have the ability to work with all levels of users, business, and IT organization to facilitate smooth test planning and coordination
  • Extraordinary verbal and written communication skills.
